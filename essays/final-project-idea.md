## Project : Better Class
The problem : UH Manoa students get stress out from what class they should take next semester and they do not want take too long to decide which can lead to lack of space for the class.
The solution : Better Class application will provide information of students at the same major, around the same credit, and grade as the users and what they are planning to take next semester. It will also provide information of students that is same major but one grade higher than the user of they class they have take last semester with the rating of 1 to 5. 1 for bad, 5 worth learning. And of course the information of class you can pick with description like what going learn, instructor, and who is taking it.

## Approach
Better Class will be login with their UH ID. When join it will ask for picture for user icon, grade, major, and credit earned. After, register, user can add class they want to take and can search for who is taking it and what else it a fun class to take but using the meet friends.
