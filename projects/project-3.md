  Clash is a special event in the game League of Legends, Player is required to make a team of five and a team name to join clash. There are 8 teams in each clash battle. Winning the clash gives all five player reward and in-game flag and trophy. Each player is required to play a role, Mid, top, bot, jungle, or support. Each role has its use and game can not win without any of them. To win the clash and get to the first place you need to beat 3 teams in a role. 
  
  My role in the Clash was the creator and MID lane. I was the one that created the team and invited 4 more people into the team. And in game I am playing as Mid Lanes, The map of the game has three lanes top, mid, bot, and between the top and mid or mid and bot are the jungles. The normal game set is one top, one mid, two bots, and one jungle. And as a mid lane, my responsebliity was to help top lane or bot lane when I have time and tell the team what is happening at mid lane at the same time. Mid Lane is important because of I am in between the two lanes, so it up to me to help them win the lane when I am doing ok in my lane.
  
  What I learn from this clash is that we need teamwork, trust, and skills to win. Trash talking or blaming others is not going to help win the game. Being calm and communicate with everyone on the team is the way to win the games and get all the awards.
