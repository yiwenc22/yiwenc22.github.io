Oceanic Institute of Hawaii VR Experience

Dive into the fascinating world of oceanic research with our immersive and interactive VR project, specifically designed for the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii. Explore the institute and its surroundings through a lifelike 360-degree video experience, and engage with interactive 3D environments powered by AI-enhanced spatial computing.

Introduction

Welcome to the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii VR Experience! This cutting-edge project was developed in collaboration with the Oceanic Institute of Hawaii to provide an unparalleled opportunity for users to explore the institute's premises and gain insights into marine research and conservation. Utilizing advanced XR technology and AI-driven interactions, this VR experience brings you closer than ever to the wonders of the ocean and the dedicated scientists working to protect it.

Key Features

  • 360-Degree Video: High-quality 360-degree footage captured with the Insta360 Pro 2 camera, creating an immersive presence at the institute
  • Interactive 3D Environments: Meticulously crafted 3D environments that recreate various on-site activities with AI-enhanced interactivity
  • Unity Platform: Built on Unity for seamless integration of 360-degree video and interactive 3D content
  • Meta Quest Compatible: Optimized for Meta Quest and Quest Pro devices, ensuring accessible high-quality experiences
  • AI-Powered Navigation: Intelligent scene transitions and interaction systems for smooth user experience

Technical Implementation

  • Insta360 Pro 2 Camera: Captured stunning, high-resolution 360-degree video content
  • Custom C# Scripts: Developed sophisticated scripts for interactions, transitions, and scene management
  • Platform: Unity with XR Toolkit integration
  • Compatible Devices: Meta Quest, Quest Pro, and PC VR systems

My Contributions

As a core developer, I played a pivotal role in bringing this VR experience to life:

  • On-Site Recording: Conducted 360-degree video capture using Insta360 Pro 2 with careful planning for optimal immersion
  • Video Processing: Processed footage using Insta360 Stitcher to create seamless, high-quality 360-degree videos
  • Unity Integration: Imported and integrated processed videos with 3D environments, ensuring smooth transitions
  • Scripting & Logic: Developed custom C# scripts for scene tracking, transition management, and user interaction systems
